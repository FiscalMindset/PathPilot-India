# Workflow: Entrepreneurship Path

## Goal
Create an entrepreneurship branch only when the user profile or prompt warrants it, and ensure it is viable, lean, and India-relevant.

## Entry Conditions
- `user_profile` suggests founder interest, income experimentation, or business-building intent
- `opportunity_map` is available

## Steps
1. Confirm entrepreneurship activation conditions.
2. Identify likely local demand areas and user-fit venture directions.
3. Produce low-cost validation loops, support ecosystem recommendations, and first-milestone business experiments.
4. Share outputs with the Roadmap Planner, Financial & Scholarship, Frontend, Backend, and Reviewer Agents.

## Exit Criteria
- entrepreneurship appears as an optional, justified branch
- early validation actions are concrete and affordable
- support resources and risks are visible

## Failure Conditions
- entrepreneurship is included without user fit
- ideas are speculative with no validation plan
