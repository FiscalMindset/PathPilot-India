# Workflow: Market Mapping

## Goal
Map viable career and entrepreneurship opportunities that fit the user profile and current India-relevant signals.

## Entry Conditions
- `user_profile` exists
- research guidance is available

## Steps
1. Determine candidate pathways based on the user profile.
2. Research relevant sectors, schemes, programs, and local opportunities.
3. Score each pathway by fit, accessibility, upside, and evidence strength.
4. Create `market_landscape`, `opportunity_map`, and `evidence_register`.
5. Send outputs to Skill-Gap, Mentor & Network, Financial & Scholarship, Roadmap Planner, Entrepreneurship, and Reviewer Agents.

## Exit Criteria
- at least one high-fit pathway exists with evidence
- rejected paths are logged with reasons
- evidence freshness and source quality are visible

## Failure Conditions
- market options are generic and not linked to the user profile
- outputs have no attributable evidence
