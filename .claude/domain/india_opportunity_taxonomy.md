# India Opportunity Taxonomy

## Purpose
Provide a non-static taxonomy of opportunity areas the system can evaluate when generating guidance for Indian users.

## Opportunity Families

### Employment-Led Paths
- digital product and engineering roles
- design and creative technology
- data and analytics
- operations and business roles
- public-sector aligned or exam-adjacent pathways where relevant
- customer success, sales, and growth roles

### Skill-First Practical Paths
- apprenticeships
- internships
- freelance service work
- project-based portfolio building
- community-led learning cohorts

### Entrepreneurship And Income Paths
- digital services for local businesses
- creator and education products
- MSME enablement services
- commerce and logistics support tools
- agritech, health, climate, or local problem-solving ventures where user fit exists
- freelancer-to-agency progression

### Support Ecosystem Paths
- scholarships and grants
- incubators and campus cells
- hackathons and competitions
- alumni and peer communities
- government or institutional schemes

## Taxonomy Rules
- this taxonomy is a discovery scaffold, not a fixed ranking
- every chosen path must be backed by user fit and evidence
- rejected categories should be logged when they materially influenced prioritization
